What I do for Fun!

The Great Outdoors

When I have time between contracts or on lieu time, I often can be found on a biketour and camping on a penninsula in Newfoundland. I love trail running on the East Coast Trail System to increase time spent looking out over the Atlantic Ocean!

I also enjoy spending time in the woods foraging for wild mushrooms, such as these belly-button hedgehog mushrooms! I also enjoy foraging for wild foods, such as blueberries, partridgeberries, scotch-lovage, and goose tongue plantain!
